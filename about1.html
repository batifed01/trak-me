Our Mission

At TrakMe we believe in creating a safer digital world for families. Our mission is to empower parents with the tools they need to protect their children from online and physical threats, manage screen time, and monitor their wards wellbeing, all while respecting privacy and promoting trust.

Our Values

Privacy-First – We never sell your data. Transparency – Parents and kids see what’s being monitored.Education Over Control – We help families build trust.

Why We Created TrakMe

With the rise of insecurity, cyberbullying, and inappropriate content, keeping loved ones safe has never been more challenging. As parents ourselves, we wanted a simple yet powerful solution that gives families peace of mind without being overly restrictive. That’s why we built TrakMe.
